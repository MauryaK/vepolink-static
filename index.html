About Vepolink

VEPOLINK, as the name signifies “Water” and “Link”. Water as a key source of existence and environment, remains treated but not monitored.

VEPOLINK provides a collection of Links that caters to the stimulation of hidden insights from Big Data. Our mission is to turn the dark data to meaningful business insights. We thrive to offer scientists, engineers, and analysts nothing but state of the art digital technologies to support them and automate industrial processes at each level.

With rise in IOT and Cloud Computing, we present VEPOLINK as a Real Time Data Acquisation, Monitoring, Reporting and analysis software for environmental data.
